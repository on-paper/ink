# Welcome to Paper Ink

Paper Ink is a minimalistic decentralized social platform that puts users in control of their data and social connections through blockchain technology.

## What is Paper Ink?

Paper Ink is a next-generation social platform that leverages Ethereum-based protocols to create a truly decentralized social experience. Unlike traditional social media, Paper Ink ensures:

- **True ownership** of your content and social graph
- **Censorship resistance** through decentralized storage
- **Interoperability** with other Web3 applications
- **Privacy-first** design with user-controlled data
